The BHMGC maintains an active directory/listing of its members. This list contains names, phone numbers, and email addresses. It is the policy of our club that the directory is strictly limited for internal use related to BHMGC events and functions. It is not to be used externally, copied, or sold for non-BHMGC activities.

Violation of this policy will result in disciplinary action determined by the BHMGC Board, up to and including expulsion.
