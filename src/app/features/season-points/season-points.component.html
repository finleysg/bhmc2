Format and Rules

  1. Players will earn and accumulate points throughout the year by playing in certain events that have been designated as Season Long Points events and are listed below.
  2. For all individual events, points will be awarded to players based on their individual score. For all 2 person team events, points will be awarded to players based on the team score with each player receiving equal points. For all 3 person or more team events, players will be awarded points based on their own individual score, not the team score. Each player on the team may receive different points depending on their own score and regardless of how their team does.
  3. In the event of ties in each specific event, there will be no tie breakers. The applicable points will be distributed amongst the tied participants.
  4. At the end of the year, the Top 20 players in points in both the Gross and Net categories will receive pro shop credit prize money based on their position and also qualify for the Jim Law Memorial Shootout.
  5. In each specific event, players will earn both Gross and Net points, however for the end of the year payouts those players that finish in the Top 20 in both Gross and Net categories will only be paid out in whichever category they finish higher in. In the event a player finishes in the same position in the top 20 in both the Gross and Net categories (i.e. 8th place gross and 8th place net) the default will be to pay them in the gross category.
